Custom Leatherwork

Introduction

Custom leatherwork can be a hobby or a business. For me, it started off as a hobby but eventually turned into a business. It is an art - crafting unique pieces while capturing functionality, durability, and personal expression. There are many techniques used to create leather goods- all of which contribute to an artists personal style and product quality. Unlike mass-produced goods, custom leatherwork allows for personalization to make each item meaningful and tailored to the needs of each individual.

Meet The Author

Nevaeh Avilez

Based in Madison County, Montana. I discovered leathercraft as a passionate hobby that quickly grew into a creative pursuit. I have always admired the timeless beauty of leather - its ability to age gracefully while reflecting craftmanship and artisitc traditions that span thousands of years. Through my work, I strive to share high-quality, hadncrafted products that honor and preserve the rich heritage of Western tradition.
